Pizza

This is a classic "Napolitano" pizza recipe. This recipe is famous for having a high hydration percentage.

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Tomato or tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Yeast
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Use as much flour you want and water should be almost the double
  2. After there is a soggy dough add yeast and a little bit of salt
  3. Let it rest for at least 6 hours, avoid low temperatures
  4. Stretch the dough and add as much tomato sauce as you want
  5. Cook in the oven 5 minutes
  6. add cheese and let it cook for another 5 minutes
  7. Enjoy your pizza
